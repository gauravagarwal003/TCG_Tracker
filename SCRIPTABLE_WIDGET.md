```txt
https://gauravagarwal003.github.io/TCG_Tracker/data/widget_summary.json
```

The payload includes:

- `day_gain_loss`
- `day_gain_loss_pct`
- `day_value_change`
- `day_cost_basis_change`
- `total_value`
- `gain_loss`
- `latest_date`
- `previous_date`
